Welcome to WenZhou Innovation Hub in Silicon Valley (欢迎来到硅谷温州创新中心）

Wenzhou Innovations Hub is a newly established startup incubator in Silicon Valley, focusing on fostering early stage technology start-ups in information technology, semi-conductor and healthcare sectors. We work to help startups maximize their growth potentials.

Our mission is to invest in disruptive innovations and build high-impact, global companies, as well as to create an international networking platform for entrepreneurs and investors.

“硅谷温创”创办于美国加州硅谷。核心成员来自就职于硅谷高科技公司的温州职业人群。成员们毕业于美国一流大学，包括斯坦佛，伯克利，哥伦比亚，UT Austin, UCLA, Rochester等高等学府， 绝大多数拥有硕士或者博士学位，工作在湾区最有潜力的信息科技公司，包括谷歌，苹果，Linkedin，Qualcomm，微软、Genentech等，行业覆盖人工智能，机器人，光纤技术，视频优化，互联网广告，通讯芯片，互联网社交、网络设备、软件开发、教育、金融、风险投资、生物医疗等多样化的领域。
